# Cheat sheet

## Commands

* `d` Delete
* `c` Change (deletes and drops into insert mode)
* `y` Yank (copy)

## Motions

* `h`, `j`, `k, `l` left, down, up, right
* `^` beginning of line
* `$` end of line
* `t<char>` till character
* `T<char>` till character (backwards)
* `f<char>` find character
* `F<char>` find character (backwards)

## Text objects

* `w` word
* `s` sentence
* `p` paragraph
* `t` tag
